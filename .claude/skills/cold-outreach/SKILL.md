# Cold Outreach Skill

## When to Use
- Creating cold email campaigns
- Building outreach sequences
- Personalizing at scale
- Writing follow-up emails
- Handling common objections

## Cold Email Structure

### Anatomy of a Cold Email

```markdown
Subject: [Personalized, curiosity-driven, 4-7 words]

Hi [Name],

[1 sentence: Personalization/research hook]

[1-2 sentences: Problem you solve]

[1 sentence: Social proof or credibility]

[1 sentence: Clear ask/CTA]

[Signature]

P.S. [Optional: Add urgency or bonus info]
```

```markdown
[Link: Watch the Demo]

[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up 3 (Day 14)
```markdown
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back -
which is totally fine.

I don't want to keep bothering you if this isn't a priority.

Should I:
1. Close your file and stop reaching out?
2. Check back in 3 months?
3. Schedule a quick call?

Just hit reply with 1, 2, or 3.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

```markdown

This is my last email.

If driver performance tracking isn't a priority right now,
I totally understand.

If anything changes, I'm always here: [email]

Best of luck with [Company].

[Your Name]
```

## Subject Line Formulas

### Curiosity-Based
```markdown
"Quick question about [Company]"
"Noticed something about [Their DSP]"
"[Mutual Connection] mentioned you"
```

### Results-Based
```markdown
"How [Similar Company] achieved [Result]"
"15% DWC improvement in 90 days"
"Saving [X] hours weekly on tracking"
```

### Personalization-Based
```markdown
"Saw your post about [Topic]"
"Congrats on [Recent Achievement]"
"[Region] DSPs are facing this challenge"
```

### Pattern Interrupt
```markdown
"This might be irrelevant, but..."
"Not another sales pitch"
"Quick favor?"
```

## Personalization Research

### Where to Find Intel
```markdown
1. LinkedIn (recent posts, job changes)
2. Company website (news, about page)
3. Google News (recent press)
4. Job postings (what they're hiring for)
5. Industry forums (what they're discussing)
```

### Personalization Hooks
```markdown
"I noticed you recently expanded to [Location]..."
"Saw your post about [Topic] - really insightful."
"Congrats on the new [Hire/Office/Contract]..."
"I know [Region] DSPs face unique challenges..."
```

## Objection Handling

### "We already have a system"
```markdown
"Totally fair - most stations do.

Quick question: Does your current system show you
which drivers need coaching in real-time?

That's usually where the biggest time savings come from."
```

### "Not a priority right now"
```markdown
"Makes sense - timing is everything.

Would it be helpful if I checked back in [3 months]
when things settle down?"
```

### "Send me more info"
```markdown
"Happy to!

Here's a 2-minute video overview: [Link]

What specifically would be most helpful to know?"
```

### "What's the cost?"
```markdown
"Depends on your setup.

For context, most stations spend $99-199/month
and save 5+ hours weekly in tracking time.

Worth a quick call to see if the math works for you?"
```

## Best Practices

### DO
- Personalize first line (prove you researched)
- Keep emails under 100 words
- Have one clear CTA
- Follow up 3-4 times
- A/B test subject lines
- Track open/reply rates

### DON'T
- Use "Hi there" (always use name)
- Write walls of text
- Attach files (triggers spam filters)
- Use "circling back" (overused)
- Send more than 5 follow-ups
- Use fake urgency

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Open Rate | 30%+ | 50%+ |
| Reply Rate | 5%+ | 10%+ |
| Meeting Rate | 2%+ | 5%+ |
| Unsubscribe | <2% | <1% |

## DO NOT
- Spam (respect opt-outs immediately)
- Lie about connections/referrals
- Use misleading subject lines
- Send without personalization
- Ignore replies (respond within 24h)
