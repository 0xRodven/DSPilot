# Landing Page Copywriter Skill

## When to Use
- Creating landing pages for DSPilot
- Writing sales page copy
- Crafting value propositions
- Designing CTAs and hero sections
- Feature announcement pages

## Copywriting Frameworks

### 1. PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

```markdown
## [PROBLEM] - Identify the pain point
"Managing delivery driver performance is a nightmare.
Spreadsheets. Manual tracking. Missed coaching opportunities."

## [AGITATE] - Make it feel urgent
"Every week without visibility costs you money.
Poor DWC scores lead to Amazon penalties.
Your best drivers quit while underperformers stay."

## [SOLUTION] - Present your answer
"DSPilot gives you real-time driver performance dashboards.
Import Amazon reports in seconds. Track DWC/IADC automatically.
Coach the right drivers at the right time."
```

### 2. AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

```markdown
## ATTENTION (Hero)
"Stop Losing Money on Poor Driver Performance"

## INTEREST (Features/Benefits)
"DSPilot imports your Amazon DWC reports automatically,
giving you instant visibility into every driver's performance."

## DESIRE (Social Proof/Results)
"Delivery stations using DSPilot see 15% DWC improvement
in the first month."

## ACTION (CTA)
"Start Your Free Trial - No Credit Card Required"
```

### 3. StoryBrand Framework

```markdown
## THE HERO (Customer)
Station managers who want their drivers to succeed

## THE PROBLEM
- External: Amazon penalties for poor DWC scores
- Internal: Feeling overwhelmed by manual tracking
- Philosophical: "Good drivers deserve good coaching"

## THE GUIDE (DSPilot)
"We've helped 50+ stations improve their DWC scores"

## THE PLAN
1. Import your Amazon reports
2. See performance dashboards instantly
3. Coach drivers with AI recommendations

## CALL TO ACTION
- Direct: "Start Free Trial"
- Transitional: "Watch Demo Video"

## SUCCESS
"Join stations achieving 98%+ DWC scores"

## FAILURE (What they avoid)
"Stop losing bonuses to preventable performance issues"
```

## Headline Formulas

```
# Value-First
"[Achieve X Result] Without [Pain Point]"
→ "Improve DWC Scores Without Spreadsheet Chaos"

# How-To
"How [Target Customer] [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]"
→ "How Station Managers Track 50+ Drivers in Minutes"

# Question
"[Rhetorical Question About Pain Point]?"
→ "Still Tracking Driver Performance in Spreadsheets?"

# Number-Based
"[Number] Ways to [Achieve Result]"
→ "3 Steps to 98%+ DWC Scores"
```

## CTA Best Practices

```markdown
## DO
- "Start Free Trial" (specific, action-oriented)
- "See It In Action" (low commitment)
- "Get Your Free Assessment" (value-focused)

## DON'T
- "Submit" (generic)
- "Click Here" (vague)
- "Learn More" (low intent)
```
