## Conversion Rules

### Blog → Twitter Thread
- Open with hook question/statement
- One idea per tweet
- Use numbers (1/, 2/, etc.)
- End with CTA + link
- 7-15 tweets optimal

### Blog → LinkedIn Post
- Personal story opening
- Bold key points for scannability
- Ask question at end for engagement
- 3-5 hashtags maximum
- 1300 characters optimal

### Blog → Instagram Carousel
- Cover slide with hook
- One concept per slide
- Visual consistency
- CTA on final slide
- 8-10 slides optimal

### Blog → Newsletter
- Personal subject line
- Story format opening
- Bullet points for key ideas
- Clear single CTA
- P.S. for engagement

## Platform Best Practices

| Platform | Length | Hashtags | Best Time |
|----------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Twitter | 280 chars | 1-2 | 8-10am, 12-1pm |
| LinkedIn | 1300 chars | 3-5 | Tue-Thu 8-10am |
| Instagram | 2200 chars | 20-30 | Mon-Fri 11am-1pm |
| Newsletter | 500-800 words | N/A | Tue/Thu 10am |

## DO NOT
- Copy-paste without adapting tone
- Use same format everywhere
- Forget platform-specific CTAs
- Ignore character limits
- Post same content same day
