## Algorithm Insights

### Twitter/X

```markdown
## What Works
- Threads (7-15 tweets) get 3x engagement
- Images/videos increase reach 150%
- Reply to comments within first hour
- Post between 8-10 AM and 4-6 PM
- Use polls for engagement

## What Hurts
- External links in main tweet (use comments)
- Hashtags (Twitter devalues them)
- Editing tweets (resets algorithm)
- Too many @mentions
```

### LinkedIn

```markdown
## What Works
- Personal stories outperform promotional
- Dwell time matters (longer posts, good formatting)
- Comments within first hour boost reach 2x
- Posting 8-10 AM weekdays
- Carousel documents get high engagement

## What Hurts
- External links (post in comments instead)
- More than 5 hashtags
- Posting on weekends
- Generic, non-personal content
```

### Instagram

```markdown
## What Works
- Carousels get 3x more engagement than single images
- First slide must stop the scroll
- Use all 10 slides
- Captions with questions increase comments
- Post between 6-9 PM

## What Hurts
- External links don't work (link in bio only)
- Low-quality images
- Inconsistent posting
- Ignoring comments
```

## Best Practices

### DO
- Lead with value, not promotion
- Use natural language (write like you talk)
- Include social proof when possible
- Respond to every comment
- Test different formats
- Repurpose across platforms

### DON'T
- Hard sell in every post
- Use the same content across platforms (adapt it)
- Ignore analytics
- Post without a CTA
- Use stock photos (authenticity wins)
- Forget to engage with others' content

## DO NOT
- Copy competitor content verbatim
- Use fake engagement tactics
- Post more than 2-3 times/day
- Ignore negative comments
- Spam hashtags
- Buy followers
