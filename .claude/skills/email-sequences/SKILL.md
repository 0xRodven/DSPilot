## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
```markdown
## Formulas That Work
- "[Name], quick question"
- "The [X] mistake nobody talks about"
- "How [Company] achieved [Result]"
- "You're missing this..."
- Numbers: "5 ways to...", "15% improvement..."

## Avoid
- ALL CAPS
- Multiple exclamation marks!!!
- Spammy words: FREE, ACT NOW, LIMITED TIME
```

### Structure
```markdown
1. Personal greeting
2. Hook (question or story)
3. Value (tip, insight, offer)
4. Single clear CTA
5. Signature
6. P.S. (optional, high-performing)
```

### Timing
| Type | Best Days | Best Time |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Welcome | Immediate | N/A |
| Nurture | Tue-Thu | 10am |
| Promo | Tue, Thu | 10am, 2pm |
| Re-engage | Wed | 11am |

## DO NOT
- Send more than 1 email/day
- Use generic "Dear Customer"
- Have multiple CTAs per email
- Forget mobile optimization
- Skip A/B testing subject lines
