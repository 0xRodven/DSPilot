# Sales Copywriter Skill

## When to Use
- Writing sales page copy
- Creating pricing page messaging
- Crafting promotional content
- Handling objections in copy
- Writing conversion-focused CTAs

## Copywriting Frameworks

### 1. AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

```markdown
## ATTENTION (Headline)
Stop the scroll. Create curiosity.
"The Driver Performance Tool Amazon DSPs Are Switching To"

## INTEREST (Problem/Solution)
Connect to their situation.
"You're spending 5+ hours weekly tracking spreadsheets.
Meanwhile, top-performing stations use real-time dashboards."

## DESIRE (Benefits/Proof)
Make them want it.
"50+ stations now achieve 98%+ DWC scores.
Average time to insights: 60 seconds."

## ACTION (CTA)
Tell them exactly what to do.
"Start Your Free Trial - No Credit Card Required"
```

### 2. PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

```markdown
## PROBLEM
Identify the pain clearly.
"Tracking driver performance in spreadsheets is killing your time."

## AGITATE
Make the pain feel urgent.
"Every week you waste on manual tracking is a week of coaching missed.
Poor performers slip through. Good drivers leave. Amazon penalties stack up.
Your competitors are already using better tools."

## SOLUTION
Present your answer.
"DSPilot gives you real-time visibility in 60 seconds.
Import Amazon reports. See every driver. Coach with confidence."
```

### 3. BAB (Before-After-Bridge)

```markdown
## BEFORE
Paint the current painful state.
"Monday morning: Download Amazon reports.
Copy into spreadsheets. Calculate DWC manually.
By Friday, you have last week's data. Too late to coach."

## AFTER
Paint the transformed state.
"Monday morning: Open DSPilot.
Every driver's performance is live.
Coach the right people before issues become patterns."

## BRIDGE
Show how to get there.
"DSPilot imports your Amazon reports in 60 seconds.
Start your free trial today."
```

## Price Presentation Psychology

### Anchoring
```markdown
## Show the "cost of inaction" first

**Without DSPilot:**
5 hours/week × $50/hour = $1,000/month in wasted time
Amazon penalties: $500-2,000/month
Lost driver bonuses: $1,000+/month

**DSPilot: $99/month**
```

### Tiered Pricing
```markdown
## Use 3 tiers

**Decoy Effect:**
Starter ($49) → Professional ($99) → Enterprise (Custom)

Middle tier should be the target.
Make it "Most Popular" or "Best Value".
```

### Risk Reversal
```markdown
## Remove the risk

- "14-day free trial"
- "No credit card required"
- "Cancel anytime"
- "Full refund within 30 days"
```

## CTA Best Practices

### Strong CTAs
```markdown
"Start Your Free Trial"
"Get Instant Access"
"See It In Action"
"Claim Your Discount"
"Join 50+ Stations"
```

### Weak CTAs (Avoid)
```markdown
"Submit"
"Learn More"
"Click Here"
"Sign Up"
"Get Started"
```

### CTA Pairs (Direct + Transitional)
```markdown
[Start Free Trial] ← Primary
[Watch 2-min Demo] ← Secondary
```

## Headline Formulas

```markdown
## Benefit-Driven
"[Achieve Result] Without [Pain Point]"
→ "Improve DWC Scores Without Spreadsheet Chaos"

## How-To
"How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]"
→ "How to Track 50 Drivers in 60 Seconds"

## Number-Based
"[Number] Ways to [Achieve Result]"
→ "3 Steps to 98%+ DWC Scores"

## Question
"[Question About Pain Point]?"
→ "Tired of Manual Performance Tracking?"

## Comparison
"The [Category] Alternative to [Competitor/Old Way]"
→ "The Smart Alternative to Spreadsheet Tracking"
```
