# Weekly Report ASCII v1

```text
WEEKLY REPORT | STATION | YEAR-WEEK

1. Headline
   - Weekly narrative in one sentence
   - State whether the week is stable, drifting, or critical

2. KPI strip
   - DWC
   - IADC
   - Risque DNR
   - Drivers actifs

3. Weekly story
   - What happened this week
   - Which defect mix drove the result
   - Whether the week is reliable enough for auto-send

4. Tier distribution
   - DSPilot-derived only
   - Count of fantastic / great / fair / poor

5. Drivers en amélioration
   - Top 5 drivers with DWC/IADC/trend

6. Drivers à risque
   - Bottom 5 drivers with DWC/risk/photos/trend

7. Alertes qualifiées
   - Severity
   - Summary
   - Link target in DSPilot

8. Priorités de coaching
   - Immediate interventions
   - Managers to brief
   - Data or mapping blockers

9. Traçabilité
   - Generated at
   - Freshness
   - Confidence
   - Reminder that driver tiers are DSPilot-derived
```
